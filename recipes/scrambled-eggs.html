Scrambled Eggs

Description

Not much to describe here. A classic quick and cheap meal. Can't go wrong.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Put a pan on medium-high heat with butter.
  2. Once the butter is melted, add in the eggs.
  3. Agitate the eggs with a spatula and continuously stir until the eggs are set.
  4. Remove from heat and enjoy.
