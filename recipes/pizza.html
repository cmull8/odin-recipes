Pizza

Description

This recipe is for the most delicious pizza you will ever consume. I have made this many times for my most distinguished guests and I have never received anything less but the highest praise. This stuff is "straight up bussin'" as the kids say.

Ingredients

  • Pre-made dough
  • Ragu Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Italian Spice blend

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees.
  2. Put pre-made dough on a baking sheet.
  3. Liberally apply that sweet Ragu to the top of the dough.
  4. Top with an entire 2 oz package of mozzarella cheese.
  5. Bake in the oven for 10 minutes till cheese is bubbling.
  6. Remove from oven and sprinkle with the Italian spice blend.
  7. Serve with more carbs and cheese.
