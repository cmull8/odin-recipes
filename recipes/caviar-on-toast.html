Caviar On Toast

Description

This dish is the image of simplicity. A true look into the duality of class. Expensive fish eggs on top and cheap, but delicious, bread on the bottom. Art imitates life.

Ingredients

  • Caviar
  • French bread

Steps

  1. Cut french bread at a bias to expose more of the surface. Place pieces on a baking sheet.
  2. Set oven to broil and lightly toast the bread.
  3. Allow the bread to cool and let the caviar come up to room temp.
  4. Use a knife or spoon to scoop caviar and spread it across the toast.
